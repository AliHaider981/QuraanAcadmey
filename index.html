About NoorPath Academy

A decade of illuminating hearts through authentic Quranic education, guided by scholarship and love for the faith.

Our Story

Founded on Faith & Excellence

NoorPath Quran Academy was founded in 2015 by a group of dedicated Islamic scholars and education professionals who shared a vision: making authentic Quranic learning accessible to Muslims everywhere in the world, regardless of location or background.

What began as a small online circle of students has grown into a thriving global academy serving over 5,000 students across 20+ countries. Every teacher at NoorPath holds an Ijazah — a sacred chain of transmission linking them directly back to the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ).

We believe the Quran is a gift meant for all of humanity, and we are honored to play a small role in guiding students on this beautiful journey of connection, understanding, and memorization.
